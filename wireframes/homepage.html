Wireframe Notes

Header: Simple and clean. Logo on the left, primary navigation on the right for easy access.

Hero: Above the fold. The goal is to immediately capture attention and state the primary value proposition. The CTA should be highly visible.

Modules: Breaks down the course content into easily scannable parts. Using a grid layout helps present the information clearly.

Testimonials: Crucial for social proof. These boxes will contain a quote, the creator's name, and their primary platform to establish credibility.

Final CTA: A larger, final section to persuade users to convert. It reiterates the value and provides a clear action button.

Overall: The layout is a standard single-column scroll for mobile-friendliness, with clear visual separation between sections.
